About

Kaleigh recently graduated in May 2014 from the University of Georgia, with a Master of Science in Internet Technology degree. In this program, Kaleigh gained project management experience, as well as honed her IT skills.

Kaleigh recieved her Bachelor of Science degree in 2012 from Georgia Southern University, in Information Technology. She specialized in Web and Multimedia, and also earned a 2nd discipline in Music Technology, as well as a Music minor.

In addition to her interests in web development and new technologies, Kaleigh has found a new interest in project management and plans on getting her CAPM certification in the near future.

When Kaleigh is not busy coding, she enjoys running, biking, and training for triathlons. She loves being outside kayaking, riding her bikes, and playing with her dogs.
